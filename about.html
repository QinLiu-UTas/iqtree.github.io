Development

The IQ-TREE software began to be developed in 2009, which was inspired by IQPNNI and PDA software.

The future release IQ-TREE 2.0 is planned to be successor of both IQ-TREE and TREE-PUZZLE.

Ongoing projects

Phylogenetic likelihood library

Further development of the phylogenetic likelihood library (Flouri et al., 2015). In collaboration with Alexandros Stamatakis and Tomas Flouri.

IQ-TREE PoMo

Development of polymorphism aware model into IQ-TREE. In collaboration with Carolin Kosiol and Dominik Schrempf.

ModelFinder

A new model selection procedure based on IQ-TREE code. In collaboration with Lars Jermiin, Thomas Wong

Heterotachy models

Development of heterotachy models in IQ-TREE, allowing for rate heterogeneity among lineages. In collaboration with Barbara Holland and Stephen Crotty.
